# Employee Portal Workflow - LMS

This document explains how employees (staff members) use the LMS for their daily professional activities.

## 1. Personal Dashboard
The **My Workplace** dashboard is the central hub for employee info.

- **Profile Overview**: See your current designation and department.
- **Task List**: View all professional tasks assigned to you by HR or Administrators. Each task displays the due date and who assigned it.

## 2. Leave Applications
Employees can apply for leave directly through the portal.

- **Applying for Leave**:
    1. Click the **Request Leave** button on the dashboard.
    2. Select the **Leave Type** (Casual, Sick, or Other).
    3. Choose the **Start** and **End** dates.
    4. Provide a brief **Reason** for the absence.
    5. Click **Submit Application**.
- **Tracking Balance**:
    - Your **Leave Balance** card shows how many days you have remaining for the current period.
    - Leave is deducted automatically once HR approves your application.
- **History**:
    - The "Leave History" table shows all past applications, their current status (Pending, Approved, Rejected), and any feedback comments from HR.

## 3. Daily Attendance
While HR usually marks attendance, employees can verify their presence status on the portal.

- **Status Check**: (Coming Soon) A summary of your monthly attendance percentage and late arrivals.

## 4. Professional Development
Employees who are also **Instructors** will have additional tabs for managing courses, student attendance, and grading.
