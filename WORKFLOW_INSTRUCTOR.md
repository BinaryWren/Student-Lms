# Instructor Workflows

The Instructor is the content creator and facilitator. Their workflows focus on course building, delivering knowledge, creating assessments, and grading.

## 1. Course Builder & Content Creation
**Goal:** Create engaging educational content.
> **Note:** Courses are **EXCLUSIVELY** created by the Institute Admin and assigned to you. You cannot create new courses yourself.
- **W1.1 Course Structure Design**:
    1.  Access **"My Courses"** from the sidebar.
    2.  Click **"Manage Content"** on an assigned course.
    3.  Create **Modules** (Chapters) to organize topics.
    4.  Create **Lessons** (Sub-sections) within Modules.
- **W1.2 Content Upload**:
    1.  **Video Lessons**: Upload video files or embed URLs (YouTube/Vimeo).
    2.  **Resources**: Attach PDFs, Slides, and supplementary files.
    3.  **CodeLab**: specific coding challenges for technical courses.
- **W1.3 Document Gallery**:
    1.  Access "Documents Gallery" (Admin/Instructor View).
    2.  Upload shared learning materials (Reference Books, Guidelines).
    3.  These items are available to all students in the institute.

## 2. Assessment Management
**Goal:** Evaluate student understanding.
- **W2.1 Assessment Creation (Quizzes & Exams)**:
    1.  Access the **"Assessments"** dashboard from the sidebar.
    2.  Click "Create Assessment" and select the **Course** you want to assign it to.
    3.  Select **Type**:
        -   **Regular Quiz**: Standard practice or graded quiz.
        -   **Pre-Assessment**: Baseline check. **Must be passed** by students to unlock course content.
        -   **Post-Assessment**: Final outcome check.
    3.  Configure settings: Time Limit, Max Attempts, and **Passing Percentage** (crucial for Pre-Assessments).
    4.  **Question Bank**: Add questions (MCQ, Short Answer).
- **W2.2 Assignment Management (Projects/Homework)**:
    1.  Create written/project Assignments.
    2.  Set Due Dates and Points.
    3.  Define simple Rubrics for grading consistency.

## 3. Classroom Delivery (Live)
**Goal:** Real-time interaction with students.
- **W3.1 Scheduling Live Classes**:
    1.  Schedule a "Live Class" session (Zoom/WebRTC integration).
    2.  Set topic, date, time, and duration.
    3.  System notifies enrolled students automatically.
- **W3.2 Conducting Class**:
    1.  Start the session as Host.
    2.  Manage participant interactions.
- **W3.3 Attendance**:
    1.  Mark attendance manually or review auto-captured attendance logs.

## 4. Grading & Feedback
**Goal:** Assess performance and guide students.
- **W4.1 Grading Submissions**:
    1.  Access the **"Gradebook"** from the sidebar (under Courses).
    2.  **Master Gradebook View**: You will see an **Accordion** list of all your courses.
    3.  **Course Overview**: Click on a course to expand it. The default **"Overview & Grades"** tab displays a comprehensive grid of all students and their results for every assignment and quiz.
    4.  **Detailed Grading**: 
        -   Switch to "Assignments", "Quizzes", etc., tabs to see list of assessments.
        -   Click **"View Results"** on any specific item to see a list of submissions.
        -   Open a submission to grade detailed work, view files, and provide **Feedback**.
    5.  **Note**: Once submitted, grades are **Locked** to prevent accidental modification.
- **W4.2 Quiz Review**:
    1.  Review auto-graded quiz results.
    2.  Manually grade short-answer questions if applicable.
- **W4.3 Grade Correction (Change Request)**:
    1.  Select a locked submission.
    2.  Click "Request Change" to propose a new grade.
    3.  Providing a reason for the change (e.g., "Calculation error").
    4.  Wait for Institute Admin approval.

## 5. Student Management & Communication
**Goal:** Mentor and support enrolled students.
- **W5.1 Attendance & Re-activation**:
    1.  **Monitor Attendance**: View daily attendance logs for students.
    2.  **Review Applications**:
        -   Receive **Re-activation Applications** from students who have been absent for 4+ days.
        -   Review the provided reason.
        -   **Forward to Admin**: Add your comment (Recommend/Not Recommend) and forward the request to the Institute Admin for final approval.
- **W5.2 Student Progress Monitoring**:
    1.  View list of enrolled students.
    2.  Check individual progress (Lessons completed, Quiz scores).
- **W5.2 Course Communication**:
    1.  Post "Course Announcements" (e.g., "Assignment deadline extended").
    2.  Reply to student queries (via Forums or direct messaging).
