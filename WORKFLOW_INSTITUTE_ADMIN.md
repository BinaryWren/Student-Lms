# Institute Admin Workflows

The Institute Admin manages the day-to-day operations of their specific educational organization. They are responsible for branding, academic structure, staffing, and student lifecycles.

## 1. Institute Setup & Branding
**Goal:** Customize the LMS to reflect the institute's identity.
- **W1.1 Branding Configuration**:
    1.  Upload Institute Logo, Favicon, and Banner images.
    2.  Set primary and secondary brand colors (Theme customization).
    3.  Configure custom domain (e.g., `lms.myinstitute.com`).
- **W1.2 General Settings**:
    1.  Navigate to **Settings -> General** in the sidebar.
    2.  Update **Institute Name**, **Contact Email**, and **Website URL**.
    3.  Configure branding colors (Primary Theme Color) with live preview.
    4.  Saving changes instantly updates the system-wide branding and sidebar identity.

## 2. Academic Structure Management
**Goal:** Define how education is delivered (Programs -> Batches -> Courses).
- **W2.1 Program (Department) Creation**:
    1.  Create Programs (e.g., "Computer Science", "Business Admin").
    2.  Assign Program Heads (optional).
- **W2.2 Batch Management**:
    1.  Create Academic Batches (e.g., "CS-2026-A", "Spring 2025").
    2.  Set Start and End dates for the batch.
    3.  Define max enrollment capacities.
- **W2.3 Course Catalog**:
    1.  Create Course shells (e.g., "Intro to Python").
    2.  Assign Courses to specific Batches.
    3.  Assign Instructors to Courses.

## 3. User Management (Staff & Students)
**Goal:** Manage the people within the institute.
- **W3.1 Instructor Onboarding**:
    1.  Send Invitations via email to prospective instructors.
    2.  Review acceptance and complete profile setup.
    3.  Review Instructor CVs and documents.
- **W3.2 Student Admission Lifecycle**:
    1.  **Lead Generation**: View inquiries from the public "Join Us" form.
    2.  **Admission Processing**: Review applications, verify documents (IDs, Transcripts).
    3.  **Enrollment**: Approve admission and assign student to a specific Batch.
    4.  **Credential Generation**: Auto-generate Student IDs and initial passwords.
- **W3.3 Bulk Operations**:
    1.  Bulk upload students/instructors via CSV.
    2.  Bulk password reset or credential repair.
- **W3.4 HR & Employee Management**:
    1.  Navigate to **HR Management -> Employees**.
    2.  **Add Employee**: Click "Add Employee", fill in details (Name, Role, Designation, Salary, etc.).
    3.  **Credential Generation**: System auto-generates **Employee ID** (`EMP-X-XXXX`) and **Password**. Credentials must be shared immediately.
    4.  **Role Assignment**: Assign "Employee" (Staff) or "HR" (Manager) roles.
    5.  **Termination**: Remove access by deleting/deactivating the employee record.

## 4. Communication & Engagement
**Goal:** Keep the institute connected.
- **W4.1 Announcements**:
    1.  Create Institute-wide announcements (e.g., "Holiday Notice").
    2.  Target announcements to specific Batches or Programs.
- **W4.2 Event Management**:
    1.  Manage the Institute Calendar (Holidays, Exam Weeks, Events).
- **W4.3 Document Gallery**:
    1.  Access the **"Documents Gallery"** section.
    2.  **Upload Material**: Click "Add Material" and select the file/link.
    3.  **Audience Targeting**: Choose between **"All Courses (General)"** or a **specific course**.
    4.  Course-specific documents are only visible to students enrolled in that specific course context.
    5.  Items uploaded here are visible via the Student Portal's Gallery.

## 5. Reports, Analytics & Moderation
**Goal:** Measure performance and oversee academic integrity.
- **W5.1 Academic Reports**:
    1.  View Gradebooks across all courses.
    2.  Identify "At-Risk" students (low attendance/grades).
- **W5.2 Financial/Admin Reports**:
    1.  View fee payment statuses (if finance module active).
    2.  Instructor activity logs.
- **W5.3 Grade Approvals**:
    1.  **Monitor Requests**: View "Pending" grade change requests from instructors for locked assignments.
    2.  **Review & Action**: Approve requests to update the student's grade or Reject with comments.
    2.  **Review & Action**: Approve requests to update the student's grade or Reject with comments.
    3.  **Audit**: View history of all approved/rejected grade changes.

## 6. Careers & Placement
**Goal:** Manage alumni success.
- **W6.1 Graduate Promotion**:
    1.  Access **"Graduate Students"** in the sidebar.
    2.  View list of students who have completed courses.
    3.  Click **"Promote to Alumni"** to move them to the Alumni Directory.
- **W6.2 Alumni Data**:
    1.  View updated employment status in the Alumni Directory. 
    2.  Edit alumni career details if needed.
