# Student Workflows

The Student is the primary consumer. Their workflows are designed for friction-free learning, clear progress tracking, and easy submission of work.

## 1. Onboarding & Profile
**Goal:** Gain access and set up the learning environment.
- **W1.1 Admission & Registration**:
    1.  Fill out the "Online Admission" application form.
    2.  **Choose Course Mode**:
        -   **Offline (On-Campus)**: Traditional learning. Login via **Student ID**.
        -   **Online Course**: Flexible learning. Login via **Email Address**.
    3.  Receive credentials via email upon approval.
        -   **Student IDs**: Offline students get standard IDs (e.g., `ACA-2026-1234`), while Online students get `ONL-` IDs (e.g., `ONL-ACA-2026-5678`).
    4.  First login: Change temporary password.
- **W1.2 Access Control**:
    -   **Online Students**: Dashboard access is streamlined to focus solely on online learning modules, quizzes, and digital certificates.
    -   **Offline Students**: Full portal access including campus-specific features like attendance tracking and community forums.

## 2. Learning & Content Consumption
**Goal:** Acquire knowledge through the platform.
- **W2.1 Dashboard & Navigation**:
    1.  View "My Dashboard" for an overview of active courses and upcoming deadlines.
    2.  Access "My Courses" to see enrolled classes.
- **W2.0 Daily Attendance & Inactivity**:
    1.  **Mark Attendance**: When accessing a course each day, you must click **"Mark Attendance"** (usually on the dashboard or course page).
    2.  **Inactivity Rule**: Identifying **4 or more consecutive days** of absence (failure to mark attendance) will result in your **Dashboard becoming INACTIVE**.
    3.  **Re-activation**:
        -   If your account is inactive, you will be redirected to an **Attendance Restoration Form**.
        -   Fill out the **Application** stating the reason for absence.
        -   The request is sent to the **Course Instructor** for review, then to the **Admin** for final approval.
        -   Once approved, you will regain access.
- **W2.1 Prerequisites**:
    1.  **Pre-Assessment**: If the course has a pre-assessment, it **must be passed** (score >= passing %) to unlock Modules and Lessons.
    2.  Take the Pre-Assessment via the "Pre-Assessments" tab or dashboard notification.
- **W2.2 Course Player**:
    1.  Enter the detailed Course View.
    2.  Navigate through Modules and Lessons via the sidebar.
    3.  **Watch Videos**: Use the custom video player (Play/Pause, Speed control).
    4.  **Download Resources**: Access PDFs and reference materials connected to lessons.
    5.  **Progress Tracking**: Mark lessons as "Complete" (or auto-complete on video finish).
- **W2.3 Document Gallery**:
    1.  Access the **"Documents Gallery"** from the sidebar.
    2.  Browse helping materials, reference books (PDFs), and useful links uploaded by the admin.
    3.  Download files or open links directly for offline study.
- **W2.4 AI Personal Tutor**:
    1.  Click the **AI Tutor** floating button (bottom-right).
    2.  **Sign in with Google** to verify your student identity and access the assistant.
    3.  Ask questions about the lecture, codebase, or general concepts.
    4.  The AI assistant creates a personalized session to clear doubts.

## 3. Assessments & Performance
**Goal:** Demonstrate knowledge and track Improvement.
- **W3.1 Taking Quizzes**:
    1.  Start a Quiz attempt within the active window.
    2.  Answer questions (MCQ, Short Answer) within the time limit.
    3.  Submit and view instant results (for auto-graded sections).
- **W3.2 Submitting Assignments**:
    1.  View Assignment details and requirements.
    2.  Upload required files (PDF, Doc, Code) before the deadline.
    3.  View Grade and Instructor Feedback once released.
- **W3.3 CodeLab (Technical)**:
    1.  Access coding challenges.
    2.  Write code in the in-browser IDE (Monaco Editor).
    3.  Run code against test cases (Judge0 integration).

## 4. Live Interaction
**Goal:** Participate in synchronous learning.
- **W4.1 Live Classes**:
    1.  View upcoming classes on the Calendar.
    2.  Click "Join" to enter the Zoom/Video session.
- **W4.2 Community**:
    1.  View Course Announcements.
    2.  Participate in QA/Discussions (if enabled).

## 5. Achievements & Records
**Goal:** Validate learning.
- **W5.1 Grades & Transcripts**:
    1.  View the "Gradebook" for a summary of performance in a course.
- **W5.2 Certification**:
    1.  Upon course completion (100% progress + Passing grade), generate the Digital Certificate.
    2.  Download PDF Certificate or share the verification link.
    2.  Download PDF Certificate or share the verification link.
- **W5.3 Career (Alumni)**:
    1.  Once promoted to Alumni, a **"Career Status"** widget appears on the dashboard.
    2.  Update your `Employment Status`, `Company`, and `Role` anytime.
    3.  This data helps the institute track placements and alumni success.
    4.  Access the Alumni Directory to network with peers.
